Alert boxes are used quite often to stand out the information that requires immediate attention of the end users such as warning, error or confirmation messages. With Bootstrap you can easily create elegant alert messages for various purposes by adding the contextual classes (e.g., .alert-success, .alert-warning, .alert-info etc.) to the .alert base class.
